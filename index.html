Projects(participant)

PI: Natural Science Foundation of Shandong Province of China

Research on Large-scale Distributed Parallel Training Platform Technology for Multimodal AI Models

Project Dates: 2022 - 2024

Research Areas: efficient computing and distributed AI; multimodal AI

Project Description: This project has conducted in-depth research on large-scale distributed parallel training technology for multimodal AI models. A multimodal unified pre-trained model with a parameter scale of tens of billions has been constructed, which is capable of performing downstream tasks such as image-text mutual checking, multimodal intelligent question answering, image generation, speech synthesis, and text continuation.
